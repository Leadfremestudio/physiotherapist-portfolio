Specialized Treatments

Sports Therapy & Training

Comprehensive rehabilitation protocols focused on returning athletes to peak performance levels safely and efficiently.

Orthopedic Manual Therapy (COMT)

Advanced hands-on techniques designed to diagnose and treat soft tissue and joint structures to modulate pain and improve range of motion.

Fascial Manipulation (Stecco)

A targeted biomechanical method to identify and resolve specific localized areas of deep fascia density causing pain or dysfunction.

Dry Needling

A specialized clinical treatment involving fine needles to deactivate muscular trigger points, relieving deep muscular pain.

IASTM

Instrument-Assisted Soft Tissue Mobilization utilizing precise ergonomic tools to effectively break down fascial restrictions.

Ergonomic Interventions

Workplace and lifestyle assessments designed specifically to optimize posture, prevent repetitive stress, and enhance performance.
